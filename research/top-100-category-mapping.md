# Top 100 App Category Mapping

Snapshot date: 2026-06-24.

This is the full row-level mapping, not examples. The free Mac chart returned 98 rows for the requested top 100; the paid overall and paid Productivity charts are mapped through rank 100.

Source boundaries:

- Free overall: parsed local Mac free chart feed.
- Paid overall: Val's Mac App Store UI screenshots plus the existing OCR-assisted transcription.
- Paid Productivity: Val's Mac App Store UI screenshots plus the existing OCR-assisted transcription.
- `Open`, `Open/Purchased`, or `View` in the price column means the storefront hid the price behind the account state.

Method:

- Category means the app's job-to-be-done, not only Apple's App Store category.
- `yes` means the core workflow can be handled by a skill/tool with transparent files and approval gates.
- `partial` means an agent can replace batch/report/content workflows but not the live UI, account, service, or resident app behavior.
- `no` means the app depends on a network, hardware, identity, real-time UI, or trust model that a coding-agent skill should not replace.
- `unknown` means the parsed name did not carry enough product signal for a defensible replacement call.
